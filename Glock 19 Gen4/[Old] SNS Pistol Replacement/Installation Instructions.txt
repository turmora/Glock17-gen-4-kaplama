Installation Tutorial for the FiveM Ready version of the Glock 19 Gen4, SNS Pistol replacement version by sergeantfranki#2708 \ https://thinlinesanctuary.com \ https://discord.gg/7UVysjf

IT IS NOT RECOMMENDED TO INSTALL ON THE SNS PISTOL SLOT, AS WE DO NOT SUPPORT IT ANYMORE! PLEASE KEEP THIS IN MIND!

Glock 19 Gen 4 installation:
 - Open the " Glock 19 Gen4 " folder.
 - Choose the colorway you want to go with.
 - Drag and Drop the folder named " thinline_glock " into your resources folder.
 - Add the resource's name: " thinline_glock " to your server's config file, in order for the resource to load up automatically.
 - You are all done! The Glock 19 is now installed on your server.				
 
 
 IF you installed the Special Engraved Edition:
- Equip the SNS Pistol, and open VMenu, or your preferred trainer, and equip the Etched Wood Grip finish in the weapon customization category.
